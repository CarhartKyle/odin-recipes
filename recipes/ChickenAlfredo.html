The Best Alfredo You've never had!

Have you always wanted to make gourmet Chicken Alfredo, but didnt know how? Well worry no more! this article will go into depth on how to create a delicious meal to feed the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breast
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Your Choice of Pasta
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Love

    • Now that you have your ingredients gathered, its time to begin!

    Instructions

    1. Cut chicken breast into bite size pieces
    2. Add olive oil to a pan on medium heat and add chicken
    3. As chicken is cooking, boil water for pasta
    4. Season Chicken according to your taste
    5. 5 minutes prior to the chicken being done, add pasta
    6. Once chicken is cooked, add alfredo sauce
    7. After sauce is heated and pasta is cooked, strain and combine!
    8. Enjoy!
