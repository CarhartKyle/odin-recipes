Mom's Spaghetti

Do you want to know how to make the famous spaghetti Eminem raves and rants about? Well look no further!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Fresh Vegetables
  • Fresh Italian sausage
  • Fresh Ground beef

    • Now that you have your ingredients gathered, let start cooking!

    1. Begin boiling water
    2. Combine meat and cook
    3. Chop vegetables
    4. Add tomato sauce to meat
    5. strain cooked pasta
    6. combine all ingredients and enjoy!
